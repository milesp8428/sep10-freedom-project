Part A

Morpholio Trace - Sketch CAD

Morpholio Trace is a drawing and design app for Apple devices that blends the ease of hand-sketching with the precision of CAD (Computer-Aided Design). It eliminates the hassle of using physical rolls of paper to trace sections, allowing you to do it digitally instead. Here is a link to their website: Morpholio Trace

Roomscan Pro Lidar Floor Plans

Roomscan Pro Lidar Floor Plans is a mobile app that lets you create a 2d and 3D floor plan. By using the built-in sensors of iPhone Pro and iPad Pro, you can obtain rapid on-site measurements. Here is a link to their website: Roomscan Pro Lidar Floor Plans

Sketchup

Sketchup Creats 3d modles. This helps architects and their clients visualize what they are making. This allows architects not to be froced in to making any big decisions.Here is a link to their website: sketchup

V-Ray

V-Ray is a tool for architects that plugs into 3D programs like SketchUp and Revit to turn basic models into high-quality, lifelike images and animations. V-Ray is like the camera for your architectural drawings. It adds real-world lighting, shadows, and materials to your digital designs, so they look like actual photos before they are even built. Here is a link to their website: V-Ray

Hardwear

  • Smart locks
    • Smart locks are things that architects will suggest that you put on your door, as they can add an extra level of protection, and for some people, it's an aesthetic choice.
    • Smart locks
  • Motorized louvered roof pergola
    • A Motorized louvered roof pergola is something that you can put outside that acts as a roof that opens and closes.
    • motorized louvered roof pergola
  • Digital pen
    • A digital pen is something that architects use to create sketches.
    • A digital pen allows you to draw on a computer or tablet. This is useful because it makes it easier to get your work out to other people.
    • A digital pen can make exact shapes, which is very important for architects when they need to be precise with what they are drawing.
    • Digital pen
  • Laser measures
    • A laser measure is something that lets you measure the distance between two points.
    • A laser measure is useful because it is very accurate and fast. This is important to architects because it helps them get an overall feel of how far things are apart from one another.
