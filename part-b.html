Solar panel windows

When you are gone you can put down your solar panel windows and they will act as solar panels on your windows. It would have a switch that you could hit to put them down or up. There could be an AI that detects when you have left the house, and it would put down the solar panels. If a light is off, they would go down in that room. They would turn to the sun to get as much sunlight as possible.

Desine App

This invention would be an app that would use AI to give you a preview of what a building would look like. You can put in the location you are going to make your building in, the amount of room you have to work with, what style you want to use, the amount of money you can use, what materials you want to use, and the app will use AI to give you sketches of both the inside and the outside of the building. It will also give you a floor plan. You would be able to make revisions until you get what you are looking for.
